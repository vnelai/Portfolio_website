About Me

My Journey

After completing high school in Greece, I moved to New York with the goal of pursuing a pre-med track in college. Driven by strong academic performance and family expectations, medicine appeared to be a stable path for growth. However, unforeseen circumstances required me to care for my two younger siblings, making it challenging to balance my studies. As a result, I redirected my efforts toward multiple jobs to provide a stable foundation for my family.

My career journey began as a shift supervisor at an adult home, where I found deep satisfaction in helping others. I then transitioned to a role at a music venue, starting as an inventory manager and advancing to director of operations for retail, events, and music operations. In this position, I honed my organizational skills and leadership abilities while cultivating a passion for data and technology. Recognizing the need for further certification to align my skills with equitable opportunities, I completed Google’s Data Analytics certification, which reignited my interest in math and opened the door to the tech field. Eager to learn more, I delved into cybersecurity and software engineering, earning several certifications through self-study. I later enrolled in a software engineering bootcamp, gaining hands-on experience with the MERN stack and project management. Additionally, I worked as an Admitting Representative in the emergency department of a well-renowned hospital, where I gained insights into the healthcare industry and finance operations. This experience taught me how to thrive in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment.

Today, equipped with knowledge in both cybersecurity and software engineering, I’m actively working on projects to deepen my skills and secure my first role in tech. My next goal is to pursue a master’s degree in computer science, completing a journey that has come full circle—from healthcare to a genuine passion for technology, driven by resilience and adaptability.
